Joey Sisk

Joey Sisk is a bassist, artist, web developer, and father living just outside of Austin, Texas. Born in 1990, Joey grew up all around the United States, from Southern California to North Carolina and New Hampshire to Oregon; he experienced American culture up and down both coasts.
After a year living and working in Yellowstone National Park, he moved down to Texas in 2011, where he worked many odd jobs while learning to play bass and meeting many amazing musicians around the Live Music Capital. These days he can be found hanging out around the North Austin area with his wife and daughter, Piper and Leta, or on the road playing great music with his friends.
